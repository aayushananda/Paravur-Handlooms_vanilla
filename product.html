Handwoven with meticulous care by the artisans of Chendamangalam. This piece embodies the resilience of a generational craft, featuring the distinct identity of Kerala's handloom heritage.

Each thread tells the story of an age-old tradition, woven on wooden charkhas and traditional looms, bringing together the purity of cotton and the elegance of authentic design.

Geographical Indication (GI) Tagged

Chendamangalam handlooms possess a prestigious GI tag, recognizing their unique quality and traditional production methods. Historically tied to the local royalty (the Paliath Achans), these weaves are famous for the puliyilakara (tamarind leaf) border and authentic kasavu detailing.
